In the area:

One of the city's oldest working-class neighbourhoods, at the foot of the St Jacques church and its garden, which is open during the day, overlooking the Canal du Midi with a panoramic view of the cathedral. The Biterrois Museum is just a stone's throw away to learn about the archaeological history of the town and the old arena, 5 minutes from the town hall and the tourist office, which is open every day from 9am to 6pm and has a wifi point.

transport:

You are 10 minutes walk from the train station and bus routes to discover the region including its beaches. 27 minutes walk from the 9 locks of Fonsérannes on the Canal du Midi where you can take a boat cruise or even have lunch.
